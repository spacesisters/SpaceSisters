Space Sisters

Two alien sisters have been kidnapped by space pirates and must now escape to travel across the galaxy to reach their home planet.

Concept

Space Sisters is a two player local co-op game. Each player controls one of the sisters. One of the sisters has the ability to pull objects towards her, while the other can push objects away from her. These abilities interact with most objects and also the other player. The physical interaction between the sisters' abilities and other objects in the game is the game's main mechanic. Furthermore, each level is procedurally generated and features various platforming tools, such as a gravity reverser, jump pads, moving platforms and so on. The abilities were purposefully designed to be complementary to encourage cooperation and still give the players multiple ways to solve any problem.
